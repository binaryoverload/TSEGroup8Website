Graph

The graph below shows graphed data for cumulative and daily COVID-19 cases and also daily flights into the UK.

By clicking on each legend entry above the chart, you can compare the different trends in data against each other.

The data is for the dates 31/01/2020 until 31/12/2020 inclusive.
